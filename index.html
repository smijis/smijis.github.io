Hi, I'm Selina.

I'm a clinical psychologist who is taking the leap into coding. For years, I’ve helped clients navigate challenges, guiding and advising but never able to directly fix the problems myself. Now, I’m ready to create my own solutions. Combining my background in psychology with a passion for mental health, I hope to build user-friendly apps that make a difference for Australians by being accessible, affordable and effective. Outside of work and coding, you’ll usually find me bouldering or playing the flute with my band — two hobbies that keep me balanced, creative, and always learning.

Join me as my worlds of psychology and technology collide.
